A Bit About Myself

With over six years of experience in challenging projects, I work on both back-end and front-end development, always aiming to deliver efficient and high-quality solutions. My journey began in Porangatu, Goiás, where I developed an early interest in science, mathematics, and everything involving creativity and technology. This passion continues to inspire me today, influencing both my work and my hobbies.

Outside of coding, I enjoy diving into science fiction topics, exploring physics concepts, watching anime, and having fun with classic games like Mortal Kombat and Super Mario. As a professional, I have experience with modern technologies such as React, Next.js, Node.js, Nest.js, SQL, and tools like Docker and AWS.

I love sharing knowledge and always strive to collaborate in agile teams, helping to build innovative solutions that meet project needs with technical excellence and creativity. If you'd like to learn more or check out some of my projects, feel free to reach out!
